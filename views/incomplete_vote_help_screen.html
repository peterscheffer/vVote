INCOMPLETE VOTE

An incomplete vote is one that is blank or incomplete. It does not count toward the election result. You can submit an incomplete vote in one or both houses.

To submit an incomplete vote, follow these instructions:

When you are on a ballot page, click next without selecting any candidates or groups. You will receive a warning message explaining that you have not completed your vote. Click the PROCEED ANYWAY button. You can do this for one or both houses.

Raise your hand to get help from a staff member
